Volunteer

Volunteers are the heart of Humane Society Yukon. From raising money, to serving on the board of directors, to helping out with the animals at the Mae Bachur Animal Shelter, there's plenty of work to be done.

At the Shelter

The Mae Bachur Animal Shelter relies on volunteers for the following activities:

  • Dog walking
  • Socialization (dogs and cats)
  • Grooming

    • Even if it's just half an hour of cuddles with one of the shelter residents, every little bit helps.

    In the Community

    Humane Society Yukon has a number of fundraising activities throughout the year that rely on volunteer support:

    Dog Wash

    Where: The Feed Store/Pet Junction
    When: Third Saturday of every month, 10am - 2pm
    What: Volunteer to wash dogs for a small fee that benefits Humane Society Yukon

    Bingo

    Where: Elk's Hall
    When: Every Wednesday evening; various shifts
    What: Bingo is the Society's primary fundraiser; volunteers are needed as bingo callers, card sellers, paymasters, and floor walkers

    Bring a completed Volunteer Application into the shelter, and the staff will be happy to help you get started.
